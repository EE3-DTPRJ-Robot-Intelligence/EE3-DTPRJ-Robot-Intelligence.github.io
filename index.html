Introduction

This is the documentation to the EE3-DTPRJ: Robot Intelligence project.

Imperial Robot Intelligence Lab is one of the leading robotics research departments of Imperial College. However additional and more extensive research hinges on additional sensors being integrated to the robots. Hence, our main goal is to address this by implementing as many necessary sensors on the robots at a relatively low cost.

The robots that we have worked extensively on are called Quickie and SLIDER.
