User Guide: Emergency Stop (E-stop)

Circuitry of emergency stop and wireless emergency stop.

GitHub Repository

Prerequisites

Hardware

  • 1 x emergency stop button
  • 1 x wireless emergency stop (adapted from a Wireless Remote Control Switch)
  • 1 x voltage step-down module (model: VMA404)

Setup

Wireless E-stop

Use interlock: connect jumper pin 1 and 2.
Relay: press one button is ON, press another button is OFF.

Connecting the wireless E-stop and the E-stop

Power line → step-down module (12V output) → (+(yellow) -(grey)) of wireless E-stop
mbed signal (red) → E-stop (pink) → wireless E-stop → mbed signal (black)

Usage

  1. In case of emergency, press the E-stop button to cut power supply.
  2. To reset the E-stop button, turn the red nob clockwise until the nob pops back up.
